Q&A

Q: Is this service for me?
A: Whether a person project at home, as simple as organizing your kitchenware, or as complex as an information server database, this service is for you. Our automated process can take any number of items from you and sort them as you wish. To add, we offer our own pre-selected options for sorted. Though general in nature, many of our clients find these options extremely useful and applicable. You are welcome to sort according to your own parameters.

Q: How is the service charged? Subscription or one time purchase?
A: We offer multiple plan options for our organization service! You can choose to pay a one-time fee for a certain number of organizations. These save you more the larger quantity you buy at once. You may also choose to opt into our organization-pro program! This is a yearly subscription service where you can receive a desired limited number of organizations per month, or our yearly unlimited organizations plan.

Q Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
A: You may cancel your subscription at any time. At the close of the subscription cycle for which you paid your subscription will end.

Q: Are refunds available?
A: Our refund policy is as follows: So long as you did not use any organizations in the package you purchased, you will be granted a full refund.
